🚀 Get your camera streaming

A few quick steps — this panel tracks your progress and tucks away when you're done.

  1. Provide the TUTK libraries Upload your Owlet APK in the Libraries card below.
  2. Sign in to Owlet Enter your email & password in the Account card.
  3. Add a camera Enter the camera's DSN and click Add & connect — it fetches the camera key automatically.
  4. Streaming Your camera goes live — point Frigate at its RTSP URL.
